7. Game Title: NotiTrivia

Playable Link: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/noti-trivia/id6764606874?platform=watch

Platform: Apple Watch (watchOS)

Description: NotiTrivia is a trivia app built exclusively for Apple Watch that delivers 2 questions per day directly to your wrist as push notifications. The whole experience lives in the notification itself — multiple choice and true/false questions show up with tappable answer buttons, so you never have to open the app. Answer correctly and your streak grows. Get it wrong or miss the 1 hour window and you lose a life. Lose all 3 lives and your streak resets. Questions rotate through a dataset of 14,000+ across a wide range of categories. The whole thing takes maybe 2 minutes of your day max. No account needed, no personal data collected.

Free to Play Status:

 Free to play
Involvement: Solo developer. Designed, built, and shipped the entire app over about 2 months. First project I've ever shipped.
